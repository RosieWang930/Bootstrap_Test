Tour an abandoned hospital

The Ellis Island Hospital, once a bustling, state-of-the-art facility, has been abandoned to the elements for over 60 years -- but you get to go inside. Take a hard hat tour to see the hospital’s autopsy room, the infectious disease ward, and all the other places you’re most likely to see movement out of the corner of your eye. It’s not a ghost tour, but it is an emotional -- and eerie -- look into the famous island’s history.
